EV Charger Installation Services

Power the future of transportation with professional EV charger installation. JB Technologies provides expert installation of Level 2 and DC fast charging stations for commercial properties, multi-family residences, and fleet operations nationwide.

Professional EV Charging Infrastructure

As electric vehicles become the standard, reliable charging infrastructure is essential. JB Technologies delivers comprehensive EV charger installation services, from site assessment and electrical upgrades to complete charging station deployment. We work with leading EV charger manufacturers to provide solutions that meet your needs, whether you're installing a single charger or deploying a network across multiple locations.

Level 2 Charging Stations

Professional installation of Level 2 EV chargers (240V) for commercial and residential applications. Perfect for workplace charging, retail locations, and multi-family properties. We handle all electrical requirements, permitting, and code compliance to ensure safe, reliable installations.

DC Fast Charging

High-power DC fast charging station installation for commercial locations and fleet operations. These stations can charge most EVs to 80% in 30-45 minutes, making them ideal for retail centers, highway rest stops, and commercial fleets requiring rapid turnaround.

Electrical Infrastructure

Complete electrical upgrades and infrastructure installation to support EV charging. We assess your existing electrical capacity, design necessary upgrades, and install dedicated circuits, panels, and transformers to support your charging network.

Network Management

Smart charging solutions with network management capabilities. Install chargers with integrated software for usage monitoring, payment processing, load management, and remote diagnostics. Perfect for commercial properties managing multiple charging stations.
