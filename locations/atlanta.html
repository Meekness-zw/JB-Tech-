Code-Driven ERRCS & DAS Delivery

IFC 510 NFPA 1221 AFRD Coordination

The City of Atlanta Fire Rescue Department and regional AHJs (DeKalb, Cobb, Gwinnett, Clayton) actively enforce ERRCS coverage during CO, with grid tests, P25 compliance, and annual recertification. We manage the entire workflow—from RF surveys and design packages to commissioning, acceptance testing, and maintenance contracts.

  • RF surveys, 20-grid/40-grid testing, DAS vs. BDA feasibility
  • Passive & active DAS design-build, P25 Class A/B BDAs
  • Fire alarm integration, annunciators, battery calculations
  • Annual inspections, remote monitoring, AHJ documentation
