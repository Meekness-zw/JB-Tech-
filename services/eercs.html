Code-Compliant Emergency Communication Solutions

When seconds count, reliable communication can mean the difference between life and death. JB Technologies specializes in designing, installing, and maintaining Emergency Responder Radio Communications Systems (EERCS) that ensure first responders maintain clear, uninterrupted communication within buildings, even in areas with traditionally poor signal coverage. Our systems meet all local, state, and federal code requirements, providing peace of mind and enhanced safety for your facility.

Code Compliance

All our EERCS installations are designed to meet NFPA, IFC, and local AHJ requirements. We ensure complete compliance with building codes and fire safety regulations, providing documentation and testing to verify system performance.

Signal Enhancement

Our bidirectional amplifier (BDA) systems extend public safety radio signals throughout your building, ensuring first responders can communicate reliably from every location, including basements, parking garages, and interior spaces.

System Testing

Comprehensive testing and commissioning services ensure your EERCS system operates at peak performance. We conduct signal strength measurements, coverage mapping, and annual compliance testing to maintain system reliability.

Maintenance & Support

Ongoing maintenance and monitoring services keep your emergency communication system operational 24/7. We provide regular inspections, preventive maintenance, and rapid response service to address any issues immediately.
