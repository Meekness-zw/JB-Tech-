Water University

This 9,380-square-foot Water University facility includes operation equipment that clients will use to learn how to minimize water use. It also has spaces to hold seminars and workshops and in one year more than 2,200 global visitors will be hosted here. Some of the highlights include interactive, touch-screen displays and a large immersion portal/video room and leading- technology before it is ready for market.
