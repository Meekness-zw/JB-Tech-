EV Charger Installation Services

Professional installation of EV charging stations with complete electrical infrastructure support. From site assessment to final commissioning, we handle every step of your EV charger installation.

Complete Installation Process

Our certified electricians and technicians follow industry best practices to ensure safe, code-compliant EV charger installations. We manage the entire process from initial site assessment through final commissioning and testing, ensuring your charging stations are ready for use.

1

Site Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of your location to determine optimal charger placement, electrical capacity, and infrastructure requirements. We assess parking layouts, electrical service capacity, and local code requirements to create a custom installation plan.

2

Design & Permitting

Detailed electrical design and engineering drawings for your EV charger installation. We handle all permitting with local authorities, ensuring compliance with electrical codes, building codes, and AHJ requirements.

3

Electrical Infrastructure

Installation of necessary electrical upgrades including service panels, dedicated circuits, conduit runs, and transformers. We ensure your electrical system can safely support the charging load and meets all code requirements.

4

Charger Installation

Professional mounting and wiring of Level 2 or DC fast charging stations. Our technicians ensure proper mounting, weatherproofing, and electrical connections. We work with leading charger manufacturers to ensure compatibility and reliability.

5

Network Integration

Connection to charging network management systems for monitoring, payment processing, and load management. We configure network settings, test connectivity, and ensure proper integration with management platforms.

6

Testing & Commissioning

Comprehensive testing and commissioning to verify proper operation, safety systems, and network connectivity. We provide documentation, user training, and ongoing support to ensure your charging stations operate reliably.
