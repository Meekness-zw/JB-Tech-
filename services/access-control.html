Access Control Services

JB Technologies offers advanced access control systems to enhance security and streamline building management. Our customizable solutions allow for secure, monitored access to facilities, integrating seamlessly with your existing infrastructure to protect your people and assets with ease.

Comprehensive Access Control Solutions

Protect your facility with intelligent access control systems that provide both security and convenience. JB Technologies designs and installs access control solutions tailored to your specific needs, from single-door systems to enterprise-wide deployments. Our systems integrate seamlessly with your existing security infrastructure, including CCTV, alarms, and visitor management systems, creating a unified security platform.

Card Access Systems

Advanced card-based access control systems supporting proximity cards, smart cards, and mobile credentials. Our systems provide secure entry management with detailed audit trails and time-based access restrictions for enhanced security.

Biometric Access

Cutting-edge biometric access control solutions using fingerprint, facial recognition, or iris scanning technology. Ideal for high-security areas requiring positive identification and eliminating the risk of lost or stolen credentials.

Keypad & PIN Systems

Cost-effective keypad and PIN-based access control for offices, server rooms, and restricted areas. These systems provide secure access management without the need for physical credentials, making them perfect for temporary access or visitor management.

Door Hardware Integration

Professional installation and integration of electronic locks, strikes, and door hardware with your access control system. We ensure proper electrical and mechanical integration for reliable, secure door operation.
