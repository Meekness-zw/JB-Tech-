Structured Cabling Services

Optimize your network infrastructure with JB Technologies structured cabling solutions. Our professional installation services deliver high-performance, organized, and scalable cabling that supports your business growth and keeps your data flowing smoothly.

Enterprise-Grade Cabling Infrastructure

A well-designed structured cabling system is the foundation of any reliable network. JB Technologies provides comprehensive structured cabling solutions that ensure optimal performance, easy maintenance, and future scalability. We design and install cable systems that support data, voice, video, and other low-voltage services, creating a unified infrastructure that adapts to your evolving technology needs.

Design & Planning

Comprehensive site surveys and custom cabling designs that meet your current needs while allowing for future expansion. We follow TIA/EIA standards and best practices to ensure optimal cable routing, proper cable management, and maximum performance throughout your facility.

Cable Installation

Professional installation of Category 5e, 6, 6A, and fiber optic cables with proper termination, labeling, and cable management. Our certified technicians ensure clean, organized installations that meet all industry standards and building codes.

Data Center Solutions

Specialized cabling solutions for data centers and server rooms, including high-density patching, overhead and underfloor cable management, and fiber optic backbones. We design systems that support high-speed data transfer and efficient cooling.

Testing & Certification

Comprehensive cable testing and certification using industry-standard equipment. We provide detailed test reports documenting cable performance, ensuring all connections meet or exceed specified standards for optimal network reliability.
